KDC's founding dataset is live: **33 project-site records** across seven
states, resolved from **183 per-source signals** by the project's two
gnomes and merged through schema-and-integrity-checked pull requests.

The depth is where it was promised: 27 records in Virginia, including the
Greater Richmond Metro — Henrico's White Oak corridor (Meta, QTS and its
17-building expansion plans, Iron Mountain, DC BLOX), Chesterfield's three
Google campuses and the Meadowville cluster, Goochland's first
Technology-Overlay-District applicant, Powhatan's permitted Page Road
campus, and Hanover — where the record includes what *didn't* happen:
one denied and one withdrawn proposal, kept as first-class `cancelled`
records. Dead proposals are signal too.

National breadth: Stargate Abilene, Meta's Hyperion in Louisiana, AWS
Project Rainier in Indiana, xAI's Colossus builds in Memphis, Microsoft
Fairwater in Wisconsin, and Google Council Bluffs.

Every value traces to a source URL; conflicting reports are kept visible
in each record's notes and its underlying signals rather than silently
resolved. Where research found nothing, [the coverage page](/coverage/)
says so — New Kent County has no data-center project, and that fact is
documented, not omitted.

Browse [the sites](/sites/), read [the schemas and provenance](/data/),
or take the YAML — it's all [CC BY 4.0](/data/).
