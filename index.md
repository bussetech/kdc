**KDC** maps US data center projects — announcements, permits, construction,
operation — as an open dataset with every fact tied to a source.

The design principle is **signal > canon**: this space is full of conflicting
reporting, so KDC collects per-source *signals* and clusters them into
resolved records with explicit confidence, staying neutral about who said
what. Dead proposals are records too — a county's denial is as much signal
as a groundbreaking.

- **[Sites](/sites/)** — every tracked project-site, by status, state, and operator
- **[Datasets](/data/)** — the raw YAML records, schemas, license (CC BY 4.0), provenance
- **[Coverage](/coverage/)** — what was surveyed, including where nothing was found

This is a [Bussetech Software Studio](https://bussetech.com) project. Its data
lives in the repo as text and the site is rebuilt from it on every change.
